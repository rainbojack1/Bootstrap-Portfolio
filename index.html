About Me

I have been coding for the last five years. I was hired at General Motors as a New College Hire in 2014. I initially started out in Java but was placed as a Front End Developer based on need. I learned the hard way in 2018 that much of what I learned was very niche to my job at GM and is not very transferrable.

I have an interest in creating things, which is why I was first attracted to front end development. In my years of being a developer, I have been introduced to database and back end development. Eventually, I decided to take a full stack developer bootcamp which has greatly solidified the skills I had acquired from working and learn many new skills.

I now have a much greater appreciation for back end development and feel a lot more confident in JavaScript development. I have a much better understanding of how database connections work and how to manipulate the data.
